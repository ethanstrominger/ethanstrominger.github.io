Hi, I'm Ethan Strominger

I am excited that you are reading my story!

In 2017, I was laid off from Oracle after having worked there for 19 years. This turned out to be a great thing. I spent time figuring out what I wanted to do. In April 2020, I completed a three month 700 hour software engineering immersive program to bring my skills up to date. This was one of the best three months of my life. More about that here. I decided I wanted to continue to have fun, and I figure the way to do this is to become an (unpaid) open source programmer and volunteer instructor. This way I could choose the most interesting projects, set my own deadlines, and maximize my contribution to the world.

I also found out that I enjoy pair programming much more than individual programming, so I will seek out projects and people I can pair with.
